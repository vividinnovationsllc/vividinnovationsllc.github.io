About Mountain Range Distribution

Mountain Range Distribution partners with brands to expand their presence across leading online marketplaces while protecting pricing, reputation, and long-term growth.

Today's eCommerce landscape is constantly evolving. Some brands are actively pursuing marketplace expansion, while others are just beginning to explore online channels or discovering their products appearing organically across digital marketplaces.

Our team works closely with brands to understand their goals and develop thoughtful marketplace strategies that align with their long-term brand vision.

Marketplace Expertise

Online marketplaces provide powerful opportunities for brands to reach new customers, but they also introduce new challenges. Our team understands the complexities of platforms such as Amazon and other major marketplaces, including the differences between first-party (1P) and third-party (3P) strategies and how brands can expand responsibly within these ecosystems.

Some brands intentionally pursue marketplace expansion, while others discover their products appearing organically through independent sellers or distribution channels. We understand these dynamics and work closely with brands to navigate them strategically.

Our focus is on helping brands build a more controlled and professional marketplace presence by improving product representation, supporting pricing integrity, and expanding online distribution in a thoughtful and sustainable way.

Our Approach

Responsible Marketplace Expansion

We work with brands to expand their presence across online marketplaces in a controlled and thoughtful way, ensuring growth happens without compromising brand standards.

Pricing Integrity

Protecting pricing structure is critical to long-term brand health. We support brands in maintaining consistent pricing and discouraging destructive marketplace price competition.

Professional Brand Representation

Product listings, imagery, and content shape how customers perceive a brand online. We focus on presenting products professionally and consistently across marketplaces.

Long-Term Partnerships

Our goal is to build long-term relationships with brands by acting as a responsible marketplace partner focused on sustainable growth rather than short-term sales.

Marketplace Execution Support

Managing online marketplaces can pull brands away from product development, customer relationships, and brand growth. We help support the day-to-day marketplace details so brands can stay focused on building the brand.
